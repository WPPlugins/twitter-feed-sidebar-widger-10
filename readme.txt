**Usage**

Once you have installed the twitter sidebar widget you need to go to your appearances menu(same place you change your themes) and under this menu you will find "widgets". Click on  "widgets" and you will see Twitter menu bar. Simply drag and drop it in the sidebar of your choice. Setup and configure your options then save, and you should be go to go. Have fun twittering!

Please report any problems with this widget by going to our website and using the support system. You may also find developer documentation available for those who need instructions on how to make modifications.

== Frequently Asked Questions == 

=Do I need any web knowledge or coding knowledge to install this widget?=
* Absolutely not. If you have learned how to use word press and can install other plugins and are familiar with how to use your wordpress website this will be very easy for you to install.

=Can this widget help with my online marketing or SEO?=
* Yes it can if you utilize it correctly. You want to make sure to keep fresh tweets posted daily or at least several times a week. Be sure a make tweets that are relevant to the search terms or keywords you want people to find you under. This is not the be all end all to SEO, but if you are doing all the other tasks necessary to rank Google will use the words in your tweets for indexing.

=How many tweets can I display at a time?=
* You can show as many as you want and you have an option to tell the widget how many
you would like to display.

=What information can I show on my website?=
* You can show everything in your twitter account such as the amount of followers your have, how many people you are following, you can show you latest tweets, your logo or avatar, choose to display links etc.

=Does it show my tweets in real time as I am tweeting them?=
* Yes it shows them in real time. If you are tweeting now you will have to refresh your
website for the to appear but they will be there once you do.
